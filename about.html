OUR STORY

More Than a Workspace.
A Movement.

HeadQ was founded on one conviction: Kerala deserves world-class workspaces that celebrate its culture, not erase it. We looked at the coworking landscape and saw spaces that could be anywhere — bland, functional, forgettable.

We built something different. Spaces that feel unmistakably like Kerala — the pace, the warmth, the ambition — while delivering the infrastructure that global teams demand.

100+ companies have already chosen HeadQ as their professional home. 2,000+ professionals walk through our doors. We're not done building.

FAQ

Everything you need to know

What does all-inclusive really mean?
It means exactly what it says — no hidden charges, no à la carte billing. Your plan covers fiber internet, conference room usage, pantry, printing, electricity, maintenance, parking, front office support, and courier handling. The only addition is GST.
Do private cabin plans include registration documents?
Yes. All private cabin, private office, team room, director cabin, and virtual office plans include corporate address support and registration documentation. The dedicated desk, individual chair, and hot desk plans include a corporate address but not registration documents.
Can I access both the Kochi and Kozhikode hubs?
Multi-hub access is available for corporate teams and selected plans. If you need presence in both cities, contact us and we'll put together the right arrangement
What are your operating hours?
HeadQ offers 24/7 building access with full generator backup across both hubs. The community management team is available during standard business hours.
How do I book a tour?
